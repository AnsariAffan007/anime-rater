Form Fields

Type

Type specifies what type of anime you're producing. Either it be a Movie or a Series with some episodes, or Chinese(Web), Music video, OVA etcetera.

Number of Episodes

For a movie, it will specify the number of parts a movie will have, for a TV series, it'll simply specify the number of episodes.

Release Year

Has a range from 1950 to 2030. For the years that have passed, as well as for those yet to come, it'll simply predict the rating. You can take it as forecasted rating for years yet to come.

Release Season

The 4 seasons of the year, with a single season spanning 3 months in ascending order.

Studio

The studio that you want to animate your Anime. Has a long list, some of which are well known, some unheard of.

Number of warnings

The Count of warnings, that the anime contained, or will contain. For example, extreme violence, cannibalism etc...

Similar Animes

The Count of animes that match with your intended anime.

Similar Mangas

The Count of mangas that match with your intended anime.

VA Count

The number of voice actors that were involved.

Staff Count

The number of staff that were involved. It can be animator, managers or any other person involved.

Genre Count

Number of genres. Generally there are 3-4, but here it has a range from 0 to 28.
